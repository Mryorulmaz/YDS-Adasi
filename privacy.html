GarageLoop Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using the GarageLoop mobile application (“App”, “Service”).

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address, name or nickname, authentication identifiers from Apple Sign-In and Google Sign-In, profile photo, and basic profile details you choose to share.
  • Content data: listings you create, item photos, descriptions, chat messages, reviews, ratings, and other content you submit in the App.
  • Location data: approximate location or city/ZIP you select, and (if you grant permission) device location to show nearby listings and suggested meeting areas.
  • Usage & device data: app version, device model, operating system version, language, in‑app screens you visit, interaction events, crash reports and performance metrics.
  • Notifications data: a push notification token (FCM/APNs) associated with your account so we can deliver alerts about messages, listings, and account activity.
  • Payments & subscriptions: information about in‑app purchases and auto‑renewing subscriptions (such as purchased product IDs, timestamps, and status). We do not receive your full payment card details; these are processed by Apple via the App Store.
  • Advertising data: limited information used by our ad partners (such as Google Mobile Ads) to show and measure ads in the App, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the core Service (accounts, listings, messaging, credits, and premium features).
  • Show nearby listings and relevant search results based on your selected or permitted location.
  • Send you notifications about messages, listing activity, account changes, and important updates.
  • Monitor and improve safety, security, fraud prevention, and abuse reporting within the community.
  • Analyze app performance and usage to fix bugs, optimize features, and plan new improvements.
  • Process in‑app purchases and subscriptions through Apple’s in‑app purchase system.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Use.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data in these limited situations:

  • Other users: When you create listings or send messages, certain information (such as your display name, profile photo, listing details, and approximate location) is visible to other users as part of the Service.
  • Service providers: We use trusted third parties for hosting, storage, analytics, notifications, authentication, and payments (for example, Firebase, Google Cloud, and Apple). They process data on our behalf under contractual safeguards.
  • Advertising partners: We may show ads using providers such as Google Mobile Ads, who may collect device and usage data to deliver and measure ads, according to their own policies.
  • Legal and safety: We may share information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of GarageLoop, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this policy.

4. Your Choices and Rights

  • Access and correction: You can view and update much of your account and profile data directly in the App.
  • Data deletion: You can request deletion of your account and related data from within the App (Settings → Delete Account) or by emailing garageloopwork@gmail.com.
  • Location: You can enable or disable location access at any time in your device’s system settings. If disabled, some location‑based features may not work.
  • Notifications: You can control push notifications in both the App settings and your device’s system notification settings.
  • Subscriptions & purchases: You can view, manage, or cancel your in‑app subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings on your device.
  • Contact us: You may contact us to request a copy of your data or ask questions about this policy.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit, access controls, and monitoring for abuse. However, no online service can be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the “Effective date” below and, where appropriate, notify you in the App or by other means. Your continued use of GarageLoop after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
